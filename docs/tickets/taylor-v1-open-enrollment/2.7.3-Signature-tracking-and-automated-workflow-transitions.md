## Product intent

Track DocuSign **status** (sent, completed, **expired**, **declined**, **voided**) and drive **ClientSpace** workflow transitions with **minimal manual intervention**—reducing reliance on Anna/Jagger to **back up** batches for resend ([2.7.2](./2.7.2-DocuSign-Workflow-via-ClientSpace.md)).

### Product requirements (Anna, April 2026)

- **Notifications:** Operations wants **visibility** when envelopes **expire**, **decline**, or are **voided** so they can **contact the client** or **restart** the workflow.
- **Automation:** Anna asked for **more automation** around resend—exact behavior TBD with ClientSpace constraints; **tasks** already generate for some failure states today (Dimple).
- **Status discipline:** Anna still wants **strict** batch progression—reps should **not** freely change statuses in ways that leave batches **stuck**; any **reset** or **resend** path must be **controlled** (e.g. proposed **Reset DocuSign** button vs. grayed states).

## Product requirements (verifiable)

- Signature events **sync** to workflow state with audit-friendly logs ([2.3.2](./2.3.2-Structured-logging-and-audit-trail-of-system-events.md)).
- Automated transitions **or** explicit tasks when envelopes fail; **no silent** stuck batches without operator visibility.
- Errors, edge cases, and operator-visible states relevant to this scope are handled or explicitly deferred with tracking.
