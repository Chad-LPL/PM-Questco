# 2.7.1.3 - Automated generation of the Benefit Guide

## Traceability
- **Asana task ID:** `1213863006807818`
- **Section:** Backlog

## Product intent

Generated from an executed client workbook, extracting data into templatized document formats. This supports Taylor V1 open enrollment orchestration. Benefits document package generation and e-signature workflow hooks.

### Why today is outsourced (April 2026 — Anna)

Historically **EB3** required building **pages per plan**—with **large master plans** (e.g. many distinct plan options), that did not scale for **1,500+** clients. A third-party enrollment firm added benefit guide production; Questco still has a **broader contract** with that vendor, so **immediate cost savings** from in-housing may be **limited**—the primary win is **cycle time** and **bringing the work in-house** over time.

### Content scope

The **benefit guide** communicates **employer-paid** benefits and broader context; **employee payroll costs** live on the **cost sheet** ([2.7.1.2](./2.7.1.2-Automated-generation-of-the-Cost-Sheets.md)).

### OMQ and broker-of-record (April 9, 2026)

When Questco is **not** the broker for an open-market plan, operations **exclude** that plan from **Questco-branded** benefit-guide content where required for **liability**—today via manual omission, vendor instructions, or agency-supplied pages inserted by the third-party guide vendor. **Open product decision:** how Taylor represents a **flag** or workflow so **regenerated** guides do not re-include excluded OMQ content ([PRD §5.8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#58-executed-workbook--documents--signature), [§8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#8-open-decisions--dependencies)). When Questco **is** the broker, agency may still provide **side-by-side** medical pages for vendor insertion—coordinate with [2.7.1.4](./2.7.1.4-Automated-generation-of-the-combined-packed.md) and [2.7.2](./2.7.2-DocuSign-Workflow-via-ClientSpace.md).

### Target

Generate **with** CBE and cost sheet for a **single review** cycle ([2.7.1.4](./2.7.1.4-Automated-generation-of-the-combined-packed.md)).

### April 9, 2026 — static vs dynamic pages

- **Conditional assembly:** Include **only** benefit chapters the client **selected** in the workbook.
- **Static library:** Many pages are **fixed** carrier/plan boilerplate (may live in **SharePoint**); **annual** updates for IRS limits (HSA/FSA), plan design changes, etc.
- **Dynamic side-by-sides:** **Medical, dental, and vision** each need **up to three** plans per page; **paginate** when more than three (e.g., seven medical → three pages). **Dental/vision** can omit “low” tier columns when clients offer fewer than three options—layout rules per Anna.
- **Table of contents** must **track dynamic page counts** when side-by-sides spill to extra pages.
- **Carrier / OMQ pages:** Some pages are **carrier-provided** PDFs—may remain **manual insert** (compliance / broker-of-record gate); third party historically built the master guide.

### April 14, 2026 — class model, sources, validation ([transcript](../../meetings/BenefitGuideDiscovery-4.14.26.md), [PRD §5.8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#58-executed-workbook--documents--signature))

- **Per-class guides:** Prefer **one benefit guide per benefit class** (each **labeled** for the class), analogous to **per-class cost sheets**—privacy (e.g., owner vs non-owner compensation), different **waiting periods**, etc. **Taylor** may generate **all** class guides; renewal reps **remove** redundant PDFs in **DocuSign** before send when a **single** guide applies to everyone.
- **Vendor assets:** Questco requested a **master guide** without the “master guide” banner on **every** page; **full** static page inventory from the vendor was still **pending** on this call.
- **Workbook vs Prism:** **Selections** (including plans not yet in Prism) come from the **workbook**; **validate** against Prism. **Material mismatch** (e.g., plan design correction after client workbook generation) → **regenerate** workbook and downstream docs.
- **Mapping:** Static pages as **PDFs** in **SharePoint**; map to **plan IDs** where possible; else **numbered static map** + **business logic** in generation (**José**). **Rates** on **cost sheet** only—not duplicated on the guide’s comparison grid. **Health Advocate:** workbook **checkbox** with other voluntaries if standalone.
- **Open:** Storing **multiple** class-specific guides in **Prism managed documents** (employee-visible) may not respect class visibility—**policy TBD** (see PRD §8).
