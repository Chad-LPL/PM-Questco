# 2.7.1.3 - Automated generation of the Benefit Guide

## Traceability
- **Asana task ID:** `1213863006807818`
- **Section:** Backlog

## Product intent

Generated from an executed client workbook, extracting data into templatized document formats. This supports Taylor V1 open enrollment orchestration. Benefits document package generation and e-signature workflow hooks.

### Why today is outsourced (April 2026 — Anna)

Historically **EB3** required building **pages per plan**—with **large master plans** (e.g. many distinct plan options), that did not scale for **1,500+** clients. A third-party enrollment firm added benefit guide production; Questco still has a **broader contract** with that vendor, so **immediate cost savings** from in-housing may be **limited**—the primary win is **cycle time** and **bringing the work in-house** over time.

### Content scope

The **benefit guide** communicates **employer-paid** benefits and broader context; **employee payroll costs** live on the **cost sheet** ([2.7.1.2](./2.7.1.2-Automated-generation-of-the-Cost-Sheets.md)).

### OMQ and broker-of-record (April 9, 2026)

When Questco is **not** the broker for an open-market plan, operations **exclude** that plan from **Questco-branded** benefit-guide content where required for **liability**—today via manual omission, vendor instructions, or agency-supplied pages inserted by the third-party guide vendor. **Open product decision:** how Taylor represents a **flag** or workflow so **regenerated** guides do not re-include excluded OMQ content ([PRD §5.8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#58-executed-workbook--documents--signature), [§8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#8-open-decisions--dependencies)). When Questco **is** the broker, agency may still provide **side-by-side** medical pages for vendor insertion—coordinate with [2.7.1.4](./2.7.1.4-Automated-generation-of-the-combined-packed.md) and [2.7.2](./2.7.2-DocuSign-Workflow-via-ClientSpace.md).

### Target

Generate **with** CBE and cost sheet for a **single review** cycle ([2.7.1.4](./2.7.1.4-Automated-generation-of-the-combined-packed.md)).
