# 2.10.2.1 - Injection of OMQ plans into renewal workbook alongside Prism-sourced plans

## Traceability
- **Asana task ID:** `1213863006807856`
- **Section:** Backlog

## Product intent

Inject **off-market quote (OMQ)** plan rows into the **same renewal workbook** Prism-driven renewal content uses, so the client sees **one artifact** with master-plan renewals and market-shopped options together ([2.5.4](./2.5.4-OMQ-plan-data-entry-and-management.md)).

**V1 input (Anna, April 2026):** OMQ details are keyed by operations into a **Questco-defined Excel template** (designed with LPL); quotes arrive from carriers/agencies in **heterogeneous PDFs**—Taylor **reads the template**, not raw carrier PDFs. **V2** may add more automated capture.

**April 8, 2026:** After template data exists, operators **regenerate** the workbook so OMQ options appear with Prism renewals. For **age-banded** quotes, injection may be **partial**—see [2.10.1.3](./2.10.1.3-Plan-attributes-carrier-rate-structure-and-core-design-elements.md).

## Product requirements (verifiable)
