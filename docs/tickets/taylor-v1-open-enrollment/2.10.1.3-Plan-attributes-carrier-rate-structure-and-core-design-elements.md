## Product intent

Rate structure (composite or age-banded). This supports Taylor V1 open enrollment orchestration. Off-Market Quote (OMQ) plan capture and inclusion in renewal and imports.

### April 8, 2026

Some OMQ proposals use **age-banded** or otherwise **non-composite** rates (e.g., dense age grids). Those **cannot always be represented** in the standard workbook; the template should mark such inputs **optional** and allow Taylor to inject **partial** data or narrative placeholders per Questco guidance ([2.10.2.1](./2.10.2.1-Injection-of-OMQ-plans-into-renewal-workbook-alongside-Prism-sourced-plans.md)).

### April 9, 2026

- **OMQ vs master rate bands:** Open-market plans use **quote-specific carrier/market rates**; they do **not** follow the client’s **Questco master rate band** model used for in-house carrier grids ([2.4.2](./2.4.2-Pre-generation-validation-of-plans-plan-groups-and-rate-band-designation.md), PRD [§5.4](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#54-rate-bands--rate-books) / [§5.7](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#57-open-market-quotes-omq)).
- **Heterogeneity:** Composite vs. age-banded (and carrier rules) vary by **state and carrier**, not only by state—design validation accordingly.
