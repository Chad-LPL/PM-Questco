- **Ingest band:** For medical, dental, and vision, Taylor needs the **L–R** data for downstream **documents and Prism mapping**—not **L alone**.
- **Column T:** Remains **exploratory** from April 8; April 9 deep dive did not promote T to required for “executed” validation.
