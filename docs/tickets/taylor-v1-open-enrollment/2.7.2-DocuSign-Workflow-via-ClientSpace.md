# 2.7.2 - DocuSign Workflow via ClientSpace

## Traceability
- **Asana task ID:** `1213863006807822`
- **Section:** Backlog

## Product intent

DocuSign initiation and tracking **through ClientSpace** for the CBE (and related envelopes per policy), preserving the **system of record** for reps while Taylor generates documents ([2.7.1.1](./2.7.1.1-Automated-generation-of-the-Client-Benefit-Elections-CBE.md)).

### Pain points (April 2026)

- **Envelope expiry** is perceived as **short** (~**48 hours** in discussion—**confirm** whether this is DocuSign configuration; Dimple to verify).
- **Anna and Jagger** are a **bottleneck:** only they (and Dimple, though reps should not route there) can **roll the batch back** to resend DocuSign when envelopes expire or fail; renewal reps **cannot** self-serve.
- **Gap:** ClientSpace may **not** expose an **expiration date field** on the stored envelope metadata (José/Dimple)—expiry may live only in DocuSign or email.

### Ideas from discovery

- Explore **Send DocuSign** availability when the batch is in **failure** states (expired/voided/declined) without encouraging unsafe manual status edits.
- Possible **“Reset DocuSign”** or equivalent action that returns the batch to a **DocuSign sent**-compatible state in a controlled way (Dimple/Jagger feasibility).

### April 9, 2026

- **Human send:** **Dimple** and **Anna** prefer a **renewal rep** (or designated operator) to **click Send** on DocuSign after **client outreach**—not fully unattended auto-send.
- **Attachments:** Envelope should include **CBE, cost sheet, and benefit guide** as **separate files** (or equivalent separate attachments), **not** one merged artifact and **not** a **zip** of everything—reduces risk the employer forwards the wrong bundle. Aligns with [2.7.1.4](./2.7.1.4-Automated-generation-of-the-combined-packed.md) and PRD [§5.8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#58-executed-workbook--documents--signature).
