# 2.5.4 - OMQ plan data entry and management

## Traceability
- **Asana task ID:** `1213863006807790`
- **Section:** Backlog

## Product intent

**Off-market quote (OMQ)** data: capture, normalization, and operator workflows so Taylor and Questco can manage plans that were **shopped outside** the standard renewal workbook path. This supports admin-console and renewal orchestration; close coupling to Prism import generation is tracked in [2.10.3.1](./2.10.3.1-Inclusion-of-selected-OMQ-plans-in-generated-Prism-import-files.md).

### As-is process (April 2026 discovery)

- The **renewal workbook** presents **current-plan renewals**—it does **not** embed open-market / OMQ options as first-class content today.
- When a client wants to shop the market, operations creates a **ClientSpace case** (agency queue); that case is **not technically linked** to the renewal batch—progress is tracked via **notes** on the batch and follow-up with agency.
- **Agency** returns quotes (rates/plan details)—often **separate PDF/Excel** from the workbook. If the client selects an OMQ plan, staff **manually** update the **CBE** and downstream steps; **new plans** are entered into **PRISM** by analysts (manual entry vs. import files for existing-plan rate updates—see Jagger/Natalia walkthrough).
- **Product direction (discovery):** Explore **injecting OMQ rows/data into the workbook** (or equivalent) so clients can compare Questco master renewals vs. open-market options in one artifact; depends on **sample OMQ templates** from prior years and **schema alignment** with PRISM/Taylor ([2.10.3.1](./2.10.3.1-Inclusion-of-selected-OMQ-plans-in-generated-Prism-import-files.md)).

### April 8, 2026

- **ClientSpace BSR:** Fields are **tracking only** (requested, assigned, received, presented, outcome)—**not** structured quote payloads. Do **not** assume the BSR can store full OMQ rate grids; the **Excel template** is the capture surface for Taylor ([2.2.2](./2.2.2-ClientSpace-API-integration-and-workflow-status-updates.md)).
- **Template + regenerate:** After temps key the OMQ template, operators **regenerate** the workbook so OMQ rows appear beside Prism-sourced renewals; downstream Prism imports follow the same path once selected.
- **Age-banded / non-composite quotes:** Some carriers return **age-banded** rates that cannot be represented fully in the standard workbook grid—template must support **optional/partial** fields ([2.10.1.3](./2.10.1.3-Plan-attributes-carrier-rate-structure-and-core-design-elements.md)).
- **Budget:** Leadership noted the original business case assumed **temp labor** for keying—keep aligned with Chris/Anna as OE scope firms up.

### April 9, 2026

- **Kickoff:** OMQ is initiated either **proactively** (strategy when renewal looks unfavorable) or when a **client requests** open market during renewal (**client service** → **shopping task** → **agency**). Agency **narrows** large carrier outputs before client-facing presentation.
- **Timing:** Often **5–10 business days** for quotes in peak season (small group); longer for **appointment** gaps, **large group**, or heavy back-and-forth. **Consultation** may last on the order of **~two weeks** while quotes are in flight—consistent with batch staying in consultation ([2.3.1](./2.3.1-Renewal-state-machine-with-defined-milestones-and-transitions.md)).
- **Roles:** **Temps** key the **Questco Excel template** for Taylor; **agency** owns strategy and client **summary** views. Taylor needs **full Prism-oriented detail** in the template, not only summary fields shown to the client.
- **Templates:** Stakeholders discussed **medical / dental / vision** as potentially **separate** templates (health is ~90% of OMQ volume). **Pending** single-template vs. split decision—align with [2.10.1.2](./2.10.1.2-Fields-aligned-to-Prism-plan-schema.md) and PRD [§5.7](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#57-open-market-quotes-omq).
- **Prism:** Sample **import header** file is in **Asana** (product)—OMQ capture should align with analyst keying and generated imports ([2.10.3.1](./2.10.3.1-Inclusion-of-selected-OMQ-plans-in-generated-Prism-import-files.md)).

### Target / V1 (confirmed 2026-04-07 — Anna)

- **Goal:** **One unified workbook** for the client: master renewal options **side by side** with open-market options, formulas calculating, so the client can compare and optionally select an OMQ plan; selections must flow back to **Prism** ([2.10.3.1](./2.10.3.1-Inclusion-of-selected-OMQ-plans-in-generated-Prism-import-files.md)).
- **Agency / underwriting** remain **out of band** (market shopping, underwriting)—that does not move into Taylor for V1.
- **V1 capture:** Carrier/agency quotes arrive in **inconsistent formats** (often **PDFs**). **No single automated parse.** Questco will use a **Questco-defined Excel template** (LPL helps design) so **temps** can key quote data consistently; **Taylor reads that template** and injects into the workbook. **V2** may add richer structured capture—“get fancy” deferred.
- **ClientSpace BSR:** Fields, checkboxes, and tasks for **OMQ tracking** (requested, shopped, presented, outcome) **already exist** but were **not used** operationally. Anna: Taylor may **not** need to replace that tracking if the team revives BSR usage; workflow status remains [2.2.2](./2.2.2-ClientSpace-API-integration-and-workflow-status-updates.md). **Admin console vs ClientSpace** for data entry is **not** a strong preference as long as reporting stays **one-stop** ([2.3.6](./2.3.6-Admin-console-providing-renewal-status-visibility-and-light-reporting.md)).
