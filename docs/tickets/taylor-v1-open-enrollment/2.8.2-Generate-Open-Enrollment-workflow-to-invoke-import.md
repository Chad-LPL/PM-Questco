## Product intent

**Scope clarification (April 10, 2026):** The SOW line “Generate Open Enrollment workflow to invoke import” maps in practice to Prism’s **Open Enrollment grid** (or equivalent UI): configuring **enrollment windows**, **workflow** selection (medical-only vs full OE vs ancillary-only vs client-specific such as **NBS**), and **dates** so employees receive the correct enrollment experience. This is **largely orthogonal** to uploading **plan** and **benefit rules** CSV imports in [2.8.1](./2.8.1-Generate-plan-setup-and-contribution-import-files.md)—those CSVs **do not** replace the grid step today.

- **Today:** After imports, analysts **manually** add/configure grid rows (per **Sue**, **LaWanda**). Operations may **bulk-import** grid assignments via Excel in some cases (e.g., ancillary-only workflow across many clients).
- **Desired direction:** After **CBE signed** / renewal finalized, **automatically** add the appropriate **OE window** row(s) and dates—or provide a **Taylor-generated** grid import file—so clients are not “left behind” without an enrollment window. **Workflow choice** is **not** always derivable from the CBE alone; may require a **mapping key** from benefits offered → workflow template, **manual** selection, or **client master** data—**open design** ([PRD §5.9](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#59-prism-import-files--manual-checkpoint), [PRD §8](../../prd/Taylor-v1-Open-Enrollment-Orchestration-PRD.md#8-open-decisions--dependencies)).
- **Taylor visibility:** [2.3.6](./2.3.6-Admin-console-providing-renewal-status-visibility-and-light-reporting.md) / reporting should help surface **grid** / window status (fetch from Prism or operational flag)—see **April 10** transcript.

Coordinate with [2.8.1](./2.8.1-Generate-plan-setup-and-contribution-import-files.md) and [2.8.3](./2.8.3-Manual-review-checkpoint-prior-to-Prism-submission.md); include **analyst** stakeholders in design.
